Genesee Valley Greenway

This is a major north-south bicycle route. It follows the west side of the Genesee River starting from Rochester's West Main Street about 100 miles southward to northern Pennsylvania.

City of Rochester Heritage Trail

From West Main Street (Susan B. Anthony Home & Museum) westward to the Erie Canal Aqueduct on Broad Street at South Avenue. This existing route has blue striping and kiosks to guide visitors near the urban center. Included along the Rochester Heritage Trail is the Anthony House & Museum

www.cityofrochester.gov/HeritageTrail/

Genesee Riverway Trail

This route stretches from Genesee Valley Park on both sides of the Genesee River all the way to Lake Ontario.
